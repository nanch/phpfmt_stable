php.fmt
=======

Thank you for upgrading.

PHP 5.5 reached end-of-life and is no longer supported.

Please, upgrade your local PHP installation to PHP 5.6 or newer.

If you do not want to upgrade your local PHP installation,
please consider installing PHPCS with PHP-CS-Fixer.

- If you like what this plugin does for you, please consider starring at https://github.com/dericofilho/sublime-phpfmt or https://github.com/dericofilho/php.tools
